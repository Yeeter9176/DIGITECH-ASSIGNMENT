COVID 19 Awareness

COVID 19 (Coronavirus) is a new disease that was discovered near the end of 2019.
Patient zero was found to be from Wuhan City, in the Hubei Province in China.
It has spread to the majority of the world and is currently classified as a pandemic.
Patient zero is believed to have contracted the disease, from an animal, and a live
animal market is believed to be the origin of the disease.
