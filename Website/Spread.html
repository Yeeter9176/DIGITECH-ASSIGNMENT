How is it spread?

The virus is similar to the flu. This is because it spreads from
person to person through droplets containing the virus, these
droplets are from when an infected person speaks, coughs or
sneezes and these droplets can either infect someone dirctly,
or they can settle on a surface and an unsuspecting person
can get into contact with them, then touch their face and
become infected. The virus can remain infectious on tehse
surfaces for days (depending on the surface) and as such
pose a threat for anyone that comes into contact with them.
